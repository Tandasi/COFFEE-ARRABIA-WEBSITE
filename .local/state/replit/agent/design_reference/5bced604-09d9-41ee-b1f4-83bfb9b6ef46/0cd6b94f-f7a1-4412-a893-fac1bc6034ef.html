Our Heritage

تراثنا العريق

A Tradition Preserved

Since 1985, Qahwa Authentica has been the heart of traditional Saudi coffee culture in Riyadh. Our family-owned establishment honors the ancient art of Arabic coffee preparation, passed down through generations.

We source our premium coffee beans from the finest regions, roasting them using time-honored techniques that bring out the rich, complex flavors that define authentic Saudi qahwa.
